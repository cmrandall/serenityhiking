About

Serenity Hiking was formed to offer people an alternative to handle mental stress as well as staying physically fit. We provide a more positive and social atmosphere than the typical gym allowing members to progress while also have fun. Overall success is met by the whole group and not just by the individual. This helps create that strong support system to really help with everyone's goals.

As a member, you will freely have the oppportunity to join any of our listed hikes. They will range in difficulty so please feel free to contact us with any questions or concerns you may have before making a decision. Our hikes are guided by experienced hikers, however, it is important to note that we are not certified guides and our group is currently free to join. Presently we are undergoing a change and partnering with another group. Overall this wil give our members exposure to even more hikes with a larger variety of focus.

We are commited to bring about change within our communiities for healthier minds and bodies
